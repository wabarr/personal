Greetings from the National Museum of Ethiopia!

I have spent the last week or so laser-scanning fossils in the collections of the museum for my dissertation research.  I have one more week remaining before I return to the USA for Christmas.

The Ethiopian National Museum  houses some of the most important fossils relevant to early human evolution.
