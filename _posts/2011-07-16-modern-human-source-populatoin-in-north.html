--- layout: post title: Modern Human source population in North Africa? date: '2011-07-16T15:23:00.000-05:00' author: Andrew Barr tags: - Morocco - Modern Human Origins - Aterian modified_time: '2011-07-16T15:23:26.872-05:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-9204176314411862946.post-4422789407159180028 blogger_orig_url: http://www.ancienteco.com/2011/07/modern-human-source-populatoin-in-north.html --- This article in early view on the PNAS website by J-J Hublin and Richard Klein argues that North African human cultures making Aterian Middle Stone Age tool industry might have been the source population for modern humans.

This area is near-and-dear to me because I was on a dig there in the summer of 2009, the year the team discovered a modern human skull.  This skull, nicknamed Bouchra, was recently featured in a special on the National Geographic Channel.
